Visualization

This visualization displays data collected for the purpose of prediciting patients with diabetes using other measurables.

The question this visualization attempts to answer is: Can the results of specific tests be used to predict diabetes?

Color indicates outcome.
Red = Positive for Diabetes
Black = Negative for Diabetes

Bar height coordinates to age.
The numbers represent BMI and they coorespond to the bar directly below them.
Number height is determined by age in order to accurately find the bar coorelated with this number.

Click the bars to sort by glucose.
After sorting,
left = low glucose
right = high glucose
